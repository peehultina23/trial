Practical 2: Cryptocurrency Price Change Calculation

Steps:
1. Take screenshots of cryptocurrency on CoinGecko.
2. Note price on first and last day.
3. Calculate percentage change and log return using formulas:
   - Final change (%) = (Pn - P1) / P1 * 100
   - Log return R = ln(Pn / P1) * 100

Example:
P1 = 87015
Pn = 68502
Final change (%) = -21.28
Log return = -23.93
